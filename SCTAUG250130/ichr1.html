About Us

The Indian Coffee House is not just a café; it's an emotion rooted in India's post-independence culture. Managed by worker cooperatives, it stands as a symbol of self-reliance and heritage. With its vintage charm and consistent taste, we welcome generations to experience our legacy of unity and simplicity.

Our Outlets

Our outlets span across India, preserving the cultural aroma of coffee and conversations. Visit us at: Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, Kolkata, Trivandrum, Bangalore, Bhopal, and dozens of towns in between.
